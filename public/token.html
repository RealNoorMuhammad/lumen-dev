Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about our crypto payment gateway

How does a customer pay with crypto? −

At the checkout, the customer selects crypto as a payment method. Then, opens his wallet and scan the QR code provided. The customer is also able to manually copy and paste the recipient's crypto wallet's address. Then the customer simply enters the required amount and confirms the transaction. See the instructions for more detail.

What is a crypto payment gateway? +

A crypto payment gateway is a service that enables businesses to accept cryptocurrency payments from customers. It processes transactions, handles conversions, and ensures secure transfer of digital assets.

What are the advantages of using cryptocurrency for online payments? +

Cryptocurrency payments offer lower transaction fees, faster international transfers, reduced chargeback risk, enhanced security, and access to a global customer base without traditional banking limitations.

How do cryptocurrency payments work? +

Cryptocurrency payments work through blockchain technology. When a customer makes a payment, the transaction is verified by the network, recorded on the blockchain, and the funds are transferred to your wallet.

What is the difference between a crypto payment gateway and a crypto wallet? +

A crypto wallet stores your digital assets, while a crypto payment gateway processes transactions between customers and merchants, handling conversions, compliance, and integration with your business systems.

How can I integrate SolPay's payment gateway into my existing e-commerce website? +

Integration is simple with our API and plugins for popular platforms. We provide comprehensive documentation, SDKs, and dedicated support to help you get started quickly.

How do I set up my payout addresses on SolPay and initiate a withdrawal? +

You can set up payout addresses in your dashboard under the 'Payouts' section. Add your wallet addresses or bank account details, verify them, and then initiate withdrawals with just a few clicks.

How long does the verification process usually take on SolPay, and what factors can influence this duration? +

Verification typically takes 1-3 business days. Factors that may affect duration include document quality, completeness of information provided, and current verification queue volume.

Where can I get hands-on experience of SolPay's invoicing system? +

You can try our demo environment or create a test account to explore all features. We also offer guided tours and training sessions for new users.

Where can I see the currencies that SolPay allows to accept for payment? +

You can view all supported cryptocurrencies in your dashboard under 'Supported Assets' or check our documentation for the most up-to-date list of accepted currencies.

How can I determine if it's legal for my business to accept cryptocurrencies via SolPay based on my country's regulations? +

We provide regulatory guidance for different jurisdictions, but we recommend consulting with local legal experts. Our compliance team can also help assess your specific situation.

What companies accept crypto payments? +

Many major companies now accept crypto payments, including e-commerce stores, service providers, and Fortune 500 companies. The list is growing rapidly across all industries.
