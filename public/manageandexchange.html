Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about crypto refunds

What's a merchant refund at SolPay? −

A merchant refund at SolPay is a feature that allows you to return cryptocurrency payments to your customers. This can be done for various reasons such as order cancellations, returns, or payment errors.

Why should a merchant use a cryptocurrency refund feature? +

Using cryptocurrency refunds provides several benefits: it maintains customer trust, complies with consumer protection laws, handles payment errors gracefully, and offers a seamless experience for both merchants and customers in the crypto ecosystem.

How does a merchant refund work at SolPay? +

Refunds work by transferring the cryptocurrency back to the customer's wallet address. You can issue refunds through your SolPay dashboard or via API, and the system handles the blockchain transaction automatically.

What's a refund form and where do I find it? +

The refund form is available in your SolPay merchant dashboard under the "Transactions" section. You can also access it via our API for automated refund processing.

What do I need to be able to issue a refund? +

To issue a refund, you need: a verified SolPay merchant account, sufficient balance in your account, the original transaction details, and the customer's wallet address where the refund should be sent.

Is there anything a customer must do to receive a refund? +

Customers don't need to take any action to receive a refund. The cryptocurrency will be automatically sent to their original wallet address. They just need to wait for the blockchain confirmation.

How is the refund amount calculated? +

The refund amount is calculated based on the original payment amount. You can choose to refund the full amount or a partial amount. The system will automatically handle the conversion if refunding in a different currency.

Is it possible to issue partial refunds? +

Yes, SolPay supports both full and partial refunds. You can specify any amount up to the original payment amount when processing a refund through your dashboard or API.
